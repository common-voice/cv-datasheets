# *[Ibibio]* &mdash; Ibibio (`ibb`)
This datasheet is for version 24.0 of the the Mozilla Common Voice *Scripted Speech* dataset 
for Ibibio (`ibb`). The dataset contains 7462 clips representing 26.72 hours of recorded
speech (11.12 hours validated) from 19 speakers.

## Language
According to [Ethnologue online](https://www.ethnologue.com/language/ibb/), Ibibio is a language of wider communication that originated in Nigeria. It belongs to the Niger-Congo language family
