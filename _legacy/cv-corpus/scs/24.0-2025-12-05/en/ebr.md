# *[Ebrie]* &mdash; Ebrie (`ebr`)
This datasheet is for version 24.0 of the the Mozilla Common Voice *Scripted Speech* dataset 
for Ebrie (`ebr`). The dataset contains 2051 clips representing 3.18 hours of recorded
speech (2.39 hours validated) from 6 speakers.

## Language
[Ethnologue online](https://www.ethnologue.com/language/ebr/) describes Ebrie also known as Tchaman or Tyaman as a stable indigenous language of Côte d’Ivoire that belongs to the Niger-Congo language family.
