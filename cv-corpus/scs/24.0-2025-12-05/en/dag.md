# _[Dagbani]_ &mdash; Dagbani (`dag`)

This datasheet is for version 24.0 of the the Mozilla Common Voice _Scripted Speech_ dataset
for Dagbani (`dag`). The dataset contains 21274 clips representing 25.39 hours of recorded
speech (9.68 hours validated) from 57 speakers.

## Language

According to [Ethnologue online](https://www.ethnologue.com/language/dag/), Dagbani is a stable indigenous language of Ghana. It belongs to the Niger-Congo language family.

## Acknowledgements

The request for the Dagbani language was officially submitted by Sadik Shahadu on October 27, 2022, on behalf of the Dagbani Wikimedians User Group, a registered not-for-profit organization and language affiliate of the Wikimedia Foundation based in Ghana and working to support the Mabia languages in Ghana. The first dataset was originally uploaded by Sadik Shahadu and the Mabia Languages Institute. The 2nd batch of the dataset was uploaded by Naafi Dasana and the Dagbani Wikimedians User Group. Through a Mozilla common Voice grant.
Other contributions occurred during a data camp organized in Yaoundé (Cameroon) in September-October 2024 by Osman Mohammed Nndow and Hadja Natuusamata Abubakari.
